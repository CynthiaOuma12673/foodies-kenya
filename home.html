Our Story

Foodies Kenya Was started in 2022 January.We are young company that aims to connect food lovers with food Service Providers. Following the COVID 19 menace one of the major sectors in the country that has been negatively impacted is the food and catering industry.The restrictions put has led to few number of people visiting hotels and food places.Private Events have also been restricted to certain number of people. This has led to many people including Chefs,cooks and other food Service Providers losing Jobs hence loose of income. It is with this background that we created Foodies Kenya.A modern Web application that allows food Service providers to list their Services on the site .In turn food lovers and those looking for food Services can book them to offer Services at their preffered location or deliver meals to them.
